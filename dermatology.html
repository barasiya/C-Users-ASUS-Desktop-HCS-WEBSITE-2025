About Us

Our Dermatology & Cosmetology Department offers the latest treatments for all your skin and beauty needs. We provide services ranging from medical dermatology to cosmetic procedures aimed at enhancing your appearance.

Our Services

  • Skin Treatments (Acne, Eczema, Psoriasis)

    • We offer specialized treatments for various skin conditions like acne, eczema, and psoriasis. Our treatments are personalized to suit your skin type and needs.

  • Hair Loss Treatment

    • We provide advanced solutions for hair restoration and scalp health, including medications, PRP therapy, and hair transplants.

  • Anti-aging Solutions (Botox, Fillers)

    • Our anti-aging treatments include Botox and dermal fillers to reduce wrinkles, fine lines, and restore youthful volume to your face.

  • Laser Hair Removal

    • We offer effective and long-lasting hair removal treatments using state-of-the-art laser technology for all skin types.

  • Facial Rejuvenation

    • Our facial rejuvenation services include chemical peels, microneedling, and advanced laser treatments to brighten and refresh your skin.

  • Cosmetic Dermatology

    • Our cosmetic dermatology services focus on enhancing your appearance with minimal downtime, offering a variety of non-invasive procedures.

  • Skin Cancer Screening

    • We provide comprehensive skin cancer screenings to detect early signs of skin cancer, ensuring your health and peace of mind.
