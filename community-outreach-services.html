Community and Outreach Services

Empowering Communities through Health, Education, and Research

Health Camps

Free Health Camps for All

Join our regular health camps aimed at providing free medical checkups and health awareness sessions. These camps are designed to serve underprivileged communities and offer vital health screenings such as blood pressure, diabetes, vision, and more.

Education and Training

Empowering Through Knowledge

We offer a wide range of free educational programs and vocational training sessions to enhance skills, improve job prospects, and promote lifelong learning. Our workshops focus on both technical and soft skills, ensuring holistic development for community members.

Medical Research

Contributing to Health Innovations

Our medical research initiatives focus on advancing healthcare by conducting studies to understand better treatment options, disease prevention strategies, and healthcare access. By participating, you help shape the future of healthcare in your community.
