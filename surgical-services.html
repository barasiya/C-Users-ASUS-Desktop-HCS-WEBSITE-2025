Minimally Invasive Surgery (Laparoscopy)

Minimally invasive surgery (laparoscopy) involves using small incisions and specialized instruments to perform surgeries. It offers faster recovery, less pain, and fewer complications than traditional surgery. We specialize in laparoscopic procedures for various conditions, including hernias and gallbladder problems.
